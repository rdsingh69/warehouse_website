Warehouse Management Software Customization Services

StockFlowTech is built to adapt to your exact cold storage operating model. Our customization services tailor workflows, reports, dashboards, integrations, and permission structures to match your site-level and company-level policies — so the software works the way your team already works.

Every warehouse is different. We configure StockFlowTech around your processes instead of forcing process changes on your team.

Common Customization Scenarios

Multi-Site Cold Storage Chain

A cold storage operator running 5 sites across a region needed consolidated reporting with site-level P&L breakdowns. We customized dashboards, cross-site stock reports, and a central finance reconciliation view.

Agricultural Co-operative with Trader Sales

A farmers' co-operative needed commission-based trader sale workflows alongside standard rent billing. We configured commission rate tables, farmer statement breakdowns, and automated settlement reports.

ERP Integration for Accounts

A warehouse operator's finance team used Tally for accounting. We built an export pipeline from StockFlowTech's payment ledger into Tally-compatible CSV format for daily reconciliation.

Custom Challan & Invoice Formats

Multiple clients required branded print templates for outward challans, rent bills, and farmer account statements. We configured print layouts matching their existing paper-based formats.
